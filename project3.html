Qualitative Research

My roles in the project: UX researcher, interviewer, poster designer.

Together with a fellow student, we conducted a qualitative research project on user expectations and concerns regarding smart gym equipment for the Advanced HCI Qualitative Research Conference at Utrecht University.

Our work earned the Best Poster Award, confirming the quality and depth of our research.

Reflection
This project taught me how to conduct in-depth user research and present findings in a clear, engaging way. A challenge we faced was getting participants to openly share their thoughts. We tackled this by using open-ended questions and creating a comfortable interview setting. Winning the Best Poster Award was a rewarding recognition of our work.
